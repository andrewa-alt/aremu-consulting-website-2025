Common Questions

What tools do I need to get started?

You likely already have what you need: Gmail, Google Sheets, maybe a CRM. We enhance what you have — no forced migrations or expensive new subscriptions.

How long does a typical automation take?

Starter automations take 1-2 weeks. Full custom systems typically 4-8 weeks from kickoff to production. Complexity depends on your workflow.

Do I need technical skills?

No technical skills required from your side. Your team just needs to use Gmail and Google Sheets. We handle all the technical build.

What happens after the automation is built?

You get full documentation, training, and 7 days of support. Plus the source code — you own the system. No ongoing subscriptions or lock-in.

What are AI agents?

AI agents are intelligent systems that make decisions and act autonomously. Unlike basic automation (if X then Y), AI agents can read emails, understand context, and decide what action to take. We build agents for claims triage, customer support, and bookkeeping that run 24/7.

Can you automate invoices from email?

Absolutely. We build pipelines that monitor your inbox, extract invoice data using AI OCR, auto-categorise by supplier, and feed straight into Google Sheets or your accounting software. Clients save 6+ hours per week just on invoice processing.

What is B2B lead scraping?

We build automated systems that crawl public business directories and websites to build verified prospect lists with emails and roles. The data feeds directly into your CRM — no more manual lead research or buying stale lists.

Can you automate social media posting?

Yes. We create automated content pipelines — YouTube uploads, TikTok posting, hashtag generation, all triggered from a single Google Sheet row. Cross-platform syndication means post once, publish everywhere. Perfect for creators and marketing teams.
