What the App Does

👨‍🍳 CHEF MODE
  • Recipe Builder
    Build meals from real ingredients. Live cost, margin %, and nutrition auto-calculated per serving.
  • Weekly Menu Planner
    Pick meals → publish when ready. Unpublished menus invisible to clients.
  • Order Management
    Cards with status workflow: pending → confirmed → prep → delivered.
  • Batch & Shopping
    Aggregated portions per meal. Shopping list grouped by category with checkboxes.
🍽️ CLIENT PORTAL
  • Public Menu Browsing
    Browse published meals with prices, calories, and macros. No login needed.
  • PIN Authentication
    4-digit PIN. No passwords, no email verification, no forgot-password flows.
  • Allergen Warnings
    Red border + ⚠️ icon on meals containing client allergens. Can't miss it.
  • Order History
    Track all past orders with color-coded status badges and totals.
🎨 DESIGN CHOICES
  • Dark Theme
    Kitchen screens get greasy. Dark theme makes smudges invisible and reduces glare.
  • Local-First
    SQLite file on disk. Works offline. Back up by copying one file.
  • Mobile-First CSS
    Scrolling through orders on a phone while moving around the kitchen.
  • No Build Step
    Vanilla JS SPA. No node_modules. No build pipeline. Just serve and go.
